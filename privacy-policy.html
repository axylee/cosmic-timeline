1. Overview

Cosmic History Timeline ("we", "our", "the site") is an interactive educational visualization available at cosmichistorytimeline.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you visit the site. We designed the site to collect as little information as technically possible.

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information directly. We do not operate a backend database of user data. We do not use Google Analytics or any comparable first-party analytics service.

The site does use your browser's local storage to remember your interface preferences (such as axis visibility toggles, zoom level, and any event edits you make). This data is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to us.
