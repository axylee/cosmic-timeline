美國獨立戰爭 American Revolution

一聲槍響在列克星頓,十三個殖民地決定脫離英國。八年戰火,華盛頓的軍隊從谷鍛營的絕望翻身到約克鎮的勝利。

A shot fired at Lexington echoed across thirteen colonies. Eight years of war, Washington's ragged army rising from Valley Forge's despair to Yorktown's surrender.

1775 年 4 月 war

Lexington & Concord·全球聽見的槍聲 Battles of Lexington and Concord

1775/4/19 英軍往 Concord 搜繳民兵武器，在 Lexington 與 Concord 與民兵交火，獨立戰爭首戰。

On Apr 19, 1775, British troops clashed with colonial militia at Lexington and Concord—the opening shots of the Revolution.

1775 年 4 月 politics

第二次大陸會議 Second Continental Congress

1775/5/10 Lexington 之後，殖民地在費城再開會，設大陸軍、發行貨幣，實質成為戰時政府。

Reconvened in Philadelphia (May 10, 1775) after Lexington, it established the Continental Army and issued currency, acting as de facto government.

1776 年 war

美國獨立·英失 13 殖民地 American Independence

7 月 4 日 13 殖民地宣布獨立。經 8 年戰爭，1781 年 Yorktown 英軍投降，1783 年《巴黎條約》英國承認美國獨立。是「第一帝國」的崩潰，但帝國重心轉印度與澳洲。

On July 4, the 13 colonies declared independence. After 8 years of war, the British surrendered at Yorktown in 1781; the 1783 Treaty of Paris recognized American independence. The 'First Empire' collapsed, but imperial focus shifted to India and Australia.

美國獨立宣言 US Declaration of Independence

北美十三殖民地宣布脫離英國統治，建立美利堅合眾國，是現代民主政治的重要里程碑。

The thirteen North American colonies declare independence from Britain, establishing the United States — a landmark of modern democracy.

1776 年 1 月 culture

Thomas Paine《Common Sense》 Thomas Paine's Common Sense

1776/1 Paine 匿名出版小冊子，以平民語言論證獨立之必要，3 個月售 12 萬份，塑造輿論。

Paine's anonymous pamphlet (Jan 1776) made the case for independence in plain language—120k copies sold in 3 months, shaping public opinion.

1776 年 8 月 war

Long Island 戰役·紐約陷落 Battle of Long Island

1776/8/27 Howe 率大軍登陸 Long Island 大敗 Washington，夜間撤退避免全軍覆沒；紐約被英占領達 7 年。

Howe's forces routed Washington on Long Island (Aug 27, 1776); a night withdrawal saved the army. British held NYC for 7 years.

1777/10/17 Burgoyne 率 6000 英軍在 Saratoga 投降，法國隨後決定公開參戰援美，扭轉戰局。

Burgoyne's surrender of ~6,000 British troops at Saratoga (Oct 17, 1777) convinced France to openly join the war as US ally.

1777 年 12 月 war

Valley Forge 過冬 Valley Forge Winter Encampment

1777-78 冬，大陸軍在 Valley Forge 苦熬，飢寒 2500 人死；Steuben 操練紀律，蛻變為正規軍。

The Continental Army endured winter 1777-78 at Valley Forge (~2,500 died of cold/hunger); Steuben's drills transformed them into a professional force.
