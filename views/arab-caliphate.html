阿拉伯帝國 Arab Caliphate

穆罕默德的啟示點燃了一場文明之火，650年間阿拉伯帝國吞並了三大洲。從麥加的一個商人到巴格達的學者之城，伊斯蘭帝國重塑了世界的宗教、政治與文化版圖。

A merchant's revelation ignited a civilization. In 650 years, the Arab Caliphate swallowed three continents, rewrote the map of faith and power, and built the world's greatest library — until the Mongols burned it down.

610 年 religion

穆罕默德首次啟示 Muhammad's First Revelation

穆罕默德在希拉山洞中接受天使吉卜利勒的啟示，古蘭經第一章降示，伊斯蘭教正式起源。

Muhammad receives the first revelation from angel Jibreel in the Cave of Hira; the first verse of the Quran is revealed — Islam officially begins.

780 年 math

花拉子密・代數學 Al-Khwarizmi — Father of Algebra

波斯數學家，著《代數學》開創代數這門學科，「Algorithm（演算法）」一詞即源自其名字拉丁化。

Persian mathematician; his book Al-Kitab al-mukhtasar creates algebra as a discipline; 'Algorithm' derives from the Latin form of his name.

965 年 science

伊本・海賽姆・光學 Ibn al-Haytham — Optics

阿拉伯學者，著《光學之書》，首次用實驗方法研究光的折射反射，被譽為現代科學方法的先驅。

Arab scholar; his Book of Optics studies light with experiment for the first time — considered a pioneer of the scientific method.
