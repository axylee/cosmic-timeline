恐龍 Dinosaurs

二疊紀末一場浩劫殺死了地球上的大多數生命，倖存者中有一群小型蜥蜴。兩億三千萬年後，牠們的子孫主宰了整個星球，直到六千萬年前的隕石終結了一切。

A cataclysm at the Permian's end wiped out most life on Earth; among the survivors, a clutch of small reptiles. Two hundred million years later, their descendants ruled the planet—until a space rock ended it all.

二疊紀大滅絕·地球最嚴重物種浩劫 Permian-Triassic Extinction

約2.52億年前，96%的海洋物種和70%的陸地脊椎動物滅絕，可能由大規模火山噴發引發，是地球歷史上最嚴重的大滅絕。

96% of marine species and 70% of land vertebrates perished — the most severe mass extinction in Earth's history, likely triggered by massive volcanic eruptions.
