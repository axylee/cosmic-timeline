重大災害 Major Disasters

從陝西地震到澳洲野火，人類在四百年間一再被自然擊倒。每場災難都改變了某個地方，也都被下一場災難遺忘了。

From Shaanxi to Australia, nature has hammered humanity relentlessly across four centuries. Each catastrophe remade a corner of the world, then faded when the next one struck.

西元前 1626 年 nature

Minoan Thera 火山爆發 Minoan Eruption of Thera

約公元前 1627 年希臘 Thera（今 Santorini）火山巨爆，可能重創 Minoan 文明，間接影響埃及氣候；青銅時代崩潰前奏。

Around 1627 BC, Thera (Santorini) volcano erupted massively, likely crippling Minoan civilization and affecting Egyptian climate.

On Jan 23, 1556, a M8.0 quake in Shaanxi killed 830,000—the deadliest earthquake in human history; loess cave dwellings collapsed en masse.

1815 年 3 月 nature

Tambora 爆發·史上最大 1815 Eruption of Mount Tambora

1815/4/10 印尼 Tambora 火山 VEI-7 爆發，人類有史以來最大火山事件，直接死 7 萬；次年成「無夏之年」。

On Apr 10, 1815, Mount Tambora (Indonesia) erupted VEI-7—the largest in recorded history; 71,000 direct deaths, triggering 1816's 'Year Without a Summer'.
