西元前 199 年 culture

中國古琴·文人音樂傳統 Chinese Guqin · Literati Music

約前 200 年中國古琴發展成熟，文人七大雅器之首；《廣陵散》《高山流水》成千年經典。

Around 200 BC, the Chinese guqin matured as the premier literati instrument; pieces like Guangling San and Gaoshan Liushui endured for millennia.

中世紀 · 1 條事件 Medieval · 1 events

1000 年 1 月 culture

Guido d'Arezzo 五線譜記譜法 Guido d'Arezzo's Musical Notation

約 1000 年義大利修士 Guido d'Arezzo 發明四線譜（後擴至五線）、Do-Re-Mi 音節；西方音樂首創系統記譜。

Around 1000 AD, Italian monk Guido d'Arezzo invented four-line staff notation (later five) and solfège (Do-Re-Mi)—the first systematic Western notation.

In 1567, Palestrina's Missa Papae Marcelli showcased counterpoint mastery; legend says it saved church polyphony from the Council of Trent.
