西元前 429 年 war

雅典大瘟疫·Pericles 危機 Plague of Athens

公元前 430-426 斯巴達封城期間，瘟疫（可能為傷寒）殺死雅典 1/4 人口，Pericles 次年殉疫。

During Sparta's siege (430-426 BC), a plague (possibly typhus) killed a quarter of Athens's population; Pericles died of it in 429.

Pericles 死於瘟疫 Death of Pericles

前 429 年秋，雅典民主領袖 Pericles 染疫而亡，之後激進民主派 Cleon 崛起，戰略趨於冒險。

Pericles died of the plague in autumn 429 BC; the radical democrat Cleon rose, pushing riskier strategies.

165 年 5 月 medicine

Antonine Plague·天花入羅馬 Antonine Plague

165-180 年羅馬帝國爆發疑似天花 / 麻疹的大瘟疫，死 500 萬（含 Marcus Aurelius 本人），動搖帝國巔峰。

A suspected smallpox/measles plague hit the Roman Empire (165-180), killing ~5M including Marcus Aurelius—shaking the empire at its peak.

541 年 6 月 medicine

Plague of Justinian·鼠疫首次世界大流行 Plague of Justinian

541-549 年 Yersinia pestis 鼠疫首次大流行，拜占庭首當其衝，全歐死 2,500-5,000 萬；重創 Justinian 復興計畫。

The first Y. pestis pandemic (541-549) devastated Byzantium and Europe, killing 25-50M and crippling Justinian's reconquest.

1492 年 9 月 medicine

哥倫布交換·新大陸疫病浩劫 Columbian Exchange · New World Epidemics

1492 後歐洲人帶天花、麻疹、流感入美洲，原住民無免疫力；一世紀內死亡 80-95%，最致命跨大陸疫病事件。

After 1492, Europeans brought smallpox, measles, and flu to the Americas, killing 80-95% of indigenous populations—the deadliest cross-continental epidemic.

1770 年 8 月 medicine

莫斯科鼠疫 1770-72 Moscow Plague of 1771

1770-72 鼠疫襲莫斯科殺 10 萬（1/3 人口）；1771 暴動逼 Catherine 大帝改革公衛。

Plague struck Moscow in 1770-72, killing 100,000 (a third of the city); the 1771 plague riot forced Catherine the Great's public health reforms.

1817 年 7 月 medicine

第一次霍亂大流行 First Cholera Pandemic

1817-24 霍亂首從孟加拉沿貿易路線擴至東亞、中東、俄羅斯；開啟 19 世紀 7 波霍亂大流行。

The first cholera pandemic (1817-24) spread from Bengal along trade routes to East Asia, the Middle East, and Russia—starting 19th-century cholera waves.

The 1889-91 flu pandemic spread from St. Petersburg along railways—the first in the modern transport era; 1M dead. Possibly a coronavirus.

1980 年 4 月 medicine

WHO 宣布天花根除 Smallpox Declared Eradicated

1980/5/8 WHO 第 33 屆大會宣布天花根除（最後自然病例 1977 索馬利亞）；人類首次根除一個傳染病，疫苗接種勝利。

On May 8, 1980, the 33rd WHA declared smallpox eradicated (last natural case: Somalia 1977)—the first disease ever eliminated by humanity.

The first SARS-CoV case emerged in Guangdong (Nov 2002); spread to 29 countries by 2003 (8,096 cases, 774 deaths), triggering WHO reforms.
