大洋洲 Oceania

一個失放流刑地成了帝國。從第一艦隊的囚犯到澳洲聯邦，再到廢除白澳政策，大洋洲在三百年間改寫了種族與民族的定義。

A penal dumping ground became an empire. From convict colonies to federation to dismantling the White Australia Policy, Oceania spent three centuries rewriting who belonged.

1788 年

第一艦隊抵 Sydney·囚犯殖民 First Fleet arrives Sydney

Arthur Phillip 帶 11 艦 1500 人（多為囚犯）抵 Sydney Cove 立 New South Wales 殖民地。澳洲原住民從此失家園，史上「白色澳洲」起點。

Arthur Phillip's 11 ships, 1,500 people (mostly convicts) reached Sydney Cove, founded New South Wales colony. Aboriginal Australians lost homeland; start of 'White Australia' era.

1840 年

Waitangi 條約·英 + 毛利 Treaty of Waitangi

英國 William Hobson 與 540+ Māori 酋長簽 Waitangi 條約。英取主權、Māori 保土地與權利。中英版本歧義至今爭議。

Britain's William Hobson signed Treaty of Waitangi with 540+ Māori chiefs. Britain gained sovereignty; Māori kept lands and rights. Translation disputes between English and Māori versions persist today.

1863 – 1944 · 7 條事件 1863 – 1944 · 7 events

1884 年

德屬新幾內亞 German New Guinea

德國取北部建德屬新幾內亞，英取南部稱英屬新幾內亞。WW1 後澳洲取代德接管整個東半，西半屬荷蘭東印度（後印尼）。

Germany took north as German New Guinea; Britain took south. After WWI Australia replaced Germany governing entire eastern half; western half was Dutch East Indies (later Indonesia).

1893 年

紐西蘭女性首先投票權 NZ first women suffrage

Kate Sheppard 領 Suffragette 運動推 Liberal 政府。9/19 立法給予所有 21 歲以上女性投票權。世界首個女性普選國，紐西蘭民主先驅形象。

Kate Sheppard's Suffragette movement pushed Liberal government. Sept 19 legislation gave all women 21+ vote — world's first universal women's suffrage. Established NZ democracy pioneer image.

1915 年

ANZAC Gallipoli·共同犧牲 ANZAC Gallipoli (shared)

紐軍與澳軍在 Gallipoli 並肩作戰共赴災難。傷亡比例尤重，紐西蘭從此「ANZAC」精神成國族基石，與澳洲共建 Bondage of blood。

NZ troops fought alongside Australians at Gallipoli, sharing disaster. Disproportionate casualties; ANZAC spirit became NZ national bedrock, blood bond shared with Australia.

1915 年

ANZAC Gallipoli·國族意識 ANZAC Gallipoli — national identity

澳紐軍團 (ANZAC) 4/25 登陸 Gallipoli 加入英軍對 Ottoman 戰線，慘敗死傷重。每年 4/25 ANZAC Day 紀念，澳洲國族意識誕生時刻。

Australia-New Zealand Army Corps (ANZAC) landed Gallipoli April 25 in British operation against Ottomans; suffered devastating losses. April 25 ANZAC Day commemorates; birth of Australian national identity.

1942 年

Kokoda 步道·阻日軍 Kokoda Track campaign — stops Japan

日軍 7 月登陸 PNG，企圖經 Kokoda 步道穿越叢林山地直取 Port Moresby。澳軍 + 民兵頑強守住，史稱「叢林版 Thermopylae」，日軍南太平洋擴張頂點。

Japanese landed PNG July, attempting to reach Port Moresby via Kokoda Track jungle-mountain. Australian troops + militia held — 'Thermopylae of the Jungle' — peak of Japanese southern Pacific expansion.

1942 年 2 月 war

新加坡淪陷 Fall of Singapore

英軍 8 萬人向日軍投降，邱吉爾稱為「英國軍史上最嚴重的災難」。新加坡被日軍占領三年半。

80,000 British Commonwealth troops surrendered to Japanese forces. Churchill called it 'the worst disaster in British military history.' Singapore was occupied by Japan for three and a half years.

1975 年

PNG 獨立 Papua New Guinea Independence

9/16 從澳洲託管獨立，Michael Somare 為首任總理。英聯邦成員，英王為國家元首。發展面對 800+ 部落語言、地形崎嶇等挑戰。

Sept 16 independence from Australian trusteeship; Michael Somare first PM. Commonwealth member with British monarch as head of state. Faces 800+ tribal languages, rugged terrain challenges.

1988 年

Bougainville 內戰 Bougainville Civil War

Bougainville 島因 Panguna 銅礦環境破壞與分潤不公引內戰。1989 Francis Ona 領叛軍封礦。9 年戰 2 萬死，1998 停火，2019 公投 98% 支持獨立。

Bougainville island civil war over Panguna copper mine pollution and inequity. Francis Ona led 1989 rebellion shutting mine. 9-year war: 20k dead; 1998 ceasefire; 2019 referendum 98% for independence.
