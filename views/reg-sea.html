東南亞歷史 SE Asia History

五百年間，殖民者來了又走，火山灰降落，獨立者奪回槍桿子。東南亞在戰火、革命與金融危機中，一次次把自己重新發明。

Colonizers came and left; volcanoes erupted; independence fighters seized the gun. In five centuries, Southeast Asia remade itself through war, revolution, and financial ruin.

1565 年

西班牙佔菲律賓·333 年 Spanish Philippines — 333 years

Miguel López de Legazpi 從墨西哥航至 Cebu 立首個西屬殖民地。後 Manila 成「Manila 大帆船貿易」樞紐連接亞美。333 年西殖民。

Miguel López de Legazpi sailed from Mexico to Cebu, founded first Spanish colony. Manila later became 'Manila Galleon Trade' hub linking Asia-Americas; 333 years Spanish rule.

1602 年

VOC 荷蘭東印度公司 Dutch East India Company

荷蘭東印度公司 (VOC) 創立，獲特許壟斷亞洲貿易、征戰權。Batavia 為總部，殖民印尼 200 年至 1799 公司倒閉、荷王室直管。

Dutch East India Company (VOC) founded with monopoly trade and military rights in Asia. Batavia HQ; colonized Indonesia 200 years until 1799 dissolution and Dutch crown takeover.

1782 年

Bangkok 建都·Chakri 王朝 Bangkok founded; Chakri dynasty

Rama I (Phra Phutthayotfa) 取代 Taksin，遷都至 Bangkok。Chakri 王朝至今 10 代延續，現代泰國政治連續性根基。

Rama I (Phra Phutthayotfa) replaced Taksin; moved capital to Bangkok. Chakri dynasty now in 10th generation; foundation of modern Thai political continuity.

法國保護國 French Protectorate

1883 年 8 月 nature

Krakatoa 火山爆發·全球氣候擾動 Krakatoa Eruption

1883/8/26-27 印尼 Krakatoa 火山爆發，聲響 4800 km 外可聞，引發海嘯殺 3.6 萬人；火山灰入平流層造成數年全球氣溫下降。

The 1883 Krakatoa eruption (Indonesia) was heard 4,800 km away, triggered tsunamis killing 36,000, and cooled global climate for years.

1893 年

泰國免被殖民 Thailand avoids colonization

Chulalongkorn (Rama V) 巧妙外交與英法平衡，犧牲邊緣領土（Laos 給法、馬來各邦給英）保住主權。東南亞唯一未被殖民國家。

Chulalongkorn (Rama V) skillfully balanced British-French; ceded peripheral territory (Laos to France, Malay states to Britain) to preserve sovereignty. Only uncolonized Southeast Asian nation.

1898 年

美西戰爭·美取菲 US takes Philippines

美西戰爭中美海軍 5/1 滅 Manila 灣西班牙艦隊。Treaty of Paris 美付西 2000 萬買下菲律賓。菲律賓人不滿引菲美戰爭 (1899-1902)。

Spanish-American War: US Navy destroyed Spanish fleet at Manila Bay May 1. Treaty of Paris: US paid Spain $20M for Philippines. Filipinos resisted, sparking Philippine-American War (1899-1902).

1942 年 2 月 war

新加坡淪陷 Fall of Singapore

英軍 8 萬人向日軍投降，邱吉爾稱為「英國軍史上最嚴重的災難」。新加坡被日軍占領三年半。

80,000 British Commonwealth troops surrendered to Japanese forces. Churchill called it 'the worst disaster in British military history.' Singapore was occupied by Japan for three and a half years.

Pol Pot took Phnom Penh April 17, founded Democratic Kampuchea. Extreme communist utopia: emptied cities, abolished money, killed intellectuals. 4-year genocide: 1.7-2.5M dead, 1/4 of population.
